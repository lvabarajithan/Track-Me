Now a days, people wanted to keep track of their places where ever they go, willing to share their location with their friends, family and loved ones and wanting
to navigate again to the place where they have visited a month ago.

With this simple and cool app, you can keep track of your location, save your location to local database and share them with your loved ones.
This application just requires GPS and active internet connection to work fine and smooth.

Now never worry about how to tell the meeting spot to your mates, just share your location in your WhatsApp group or in mail or what so ever.
Just a click away to track, locate and share your location.
